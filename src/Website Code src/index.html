Welcome to the future of textbooks

Every object, person, and thing has a story behind it.  It is our responsibility as humans to seek out these stories and understand the importance of learning how and why things are created. However, it can be difficult to know where to start looking for these stories. This is where Open SoARce comes to the rescue! Instead of tasking one person or team to generate the content and share the hidden stories of things that may appear in your textbook, or everyday life, we thought we could outsource this so that anyone can contribute to bringing to light stories that may not be as well known. So, let's get started! Scroll down to see how you can help out.

How does it work?

1. Upload a Tracking Image

The Open SoARce textbook requires an image to trigger the augmented overlay of the application. Upload an image that you would like to use for this purpose.

2. Add AR Overlay Content

Add the information that you'd like to add to the image you've just uploaded. This will be uploaded to a database of Open Source information so that you can help contribute to sharing hidden stories across the community!

3. Choose what you want to display

Customize what you'd like your AR to look like! Open SoARce accepts text, images, and webpages as sources you can add to any image! See below for demos.

About Open SoARce

Open SoARce is a project created as part a capstone project for the Media Studies Senior Seminar course at Pitzer College. The aim of the project is to bring to light to some of the hidden stories behind the concepts we learn in school.
